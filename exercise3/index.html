XML

An XML sitemap shows the content of your site and how it links together. It is important to help inform search engings of the content on your site so that this information can be found more easily during a search. Typically, a search engine will automatically look at your site's information, but a site map ensures that all of your content is properly read and reported. An automated search might miss part of the site or not see how certain information is connected. A sitemap tells the crawler exactly what is on your site and how it should be connected.

Design Practices and Analytics

Accessibility

Tab order is the order in which a user can utilize the tab key for navigating a site. It is an important part of accessibility because not every user can easily use a mouse or touch pad for manuevering through a site. Tab order can easily be altered through the use of tabindex.


WCAG2 is the 2 edition of web content accessibility guidelines and WAI is the web accessibility initiative. WCAG2 is intended to "make content accessible to a wider range of people with disabilities, including blindness and low vision, deafness and hearing loss, learning disabilities, cognitive limitations, limited movement, speech disabilities, photosensitivity and combinations of these. " (reference). Following these guidelines can make the web more useful for all people. It includes a wide variety of items such as color, captioning, text size and timing. The WAI develops standards that are intended to make WCAG2.0 implementation easier.


Color contrast analyzers help determine the visibility and readibility of a site. It is important for people with color blindness and other visual impairements. Otherwise, they may not be able to use your site and you could lose important traffic.

Usability

User-centered design is the process of designing based on how end users will view the system. Ideally, users will not need to change their behaviours in order to use your design. Rather, your design will conform to user expectations.


Usability is the interactive user experience, i.e. can a user discover information or use your site as intended without needing to make changes to their behavior or learn new ways of doing things.


Brand awareness the ability to recognize your brand. Does your site increase brand awareness through the use of colors and logos? Does the information displayed link back to company goals and make users aware of those things. Does the site drive visitors to want brand purchases?

SEO

Links signals are signs that tell a search engine how important your website is. Search engines use the number of links as one metric to determine if a site is popular and increases page rankings accordingly.


Google Analytics is a free way to analyze your web site & traffic. Yes, it is important for SEO since traffic is the most important part of most sites and Google is the largest search engine in existence currently.It's important to understand who is visiting your site and for how long and what things you might change to improve those statistics as well as to make your site more appealing to Google.


Social Media optimization is optimizing your site so that your social media presence can be as large as possible. Social media presence plays a role in search engine rankings, though it's unclear how large that role is. The other important aspect of social media optimization is to make your site sharing friendly. You can increase your online presence by optimizing the ways users can share your site through things such as article sharing, facebook comments and sharing links.


Webmaster tools are tools for site owners that help them maintain and control their site. This includes site optimization, checking the indexing status, and discovering issues.

Google Analytics

Google Analytics offers 10 different types of reports that each have a variety of features. These reports include:

  • Audience Reports - to learn about your users
  • Advertising Reports - information about your advertising
  • Acquisition Reports - information about user conversion
  • Behavior Reports - information about user interaction
  • Conversion Reports - analytics on how your various channels are converting users
  • Real-Time Reports - real-time site information
  • Flow Reports - how do users interact with your site
  • Data Freshness, Unsampled Reports, & Roll-up Reports - uses Analytics 360 only for additional reports.

Report information can help to fine tune your site by showing who is using it and what they do when visiting your site. It can help determine how and why customers visit your site and what changes you need to make to get your important information viewed by them.


There are a variety of tools that can be used with Google Analytics.

  • Google Adsense
  • Google Cloud
  • Google Search
  • Data Studio
